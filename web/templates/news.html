üìñ How to Use Unusual Options Flow

What Makes Flow "Unusual"?
  • Vol/OI Ratio > 3: Volume much higher than open interest = NEW positions being opened (not just trading existing ones)
  • Large Premium: $500K+ in a single strike = Institutional/whale money
  • OTM Bets: Out-of-the-money options = Directional speculation
How to Trade This
Bullish Flow (Calls):

Large call buying with high Vol/OI suggests someone expects the stock to go UP.

  • Consider buying calls or call spreads
  • Look for confirmation in price action
  • Watch the same expiration as the flow
Bearish Flow (Puts):

Large put buying with high Vol/OI suggests someone expects the stock to go DOWN.

  • Consider buying puts or put spreads
  • Could also be hedging - check if stock is up big
  • More reliable near earnings/events
